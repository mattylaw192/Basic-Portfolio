"'You miss 100% of the shots you don't take' - Wayne Gretzy - Matt Lawson"

Born in the corn jungles of Illinois, Matt was surrounded by vegetables and subpar sports teams from day 1. The lack of success that surrounded him was frustrating. Matt was a "winner", and knew he had to get out.

The next logical step for Matt was to move to a different city - one with a winning attitude. Matt pulled a "Lebron" by leaving a city devoid of opportunity and relocated to Boston. 3 World Series, 5 Super Bowls, 1 NBA Championship, and 1 Stanley Cup later, Matt is still in New England, basking in the "winning glory".

Matt has mimiced the winning ways of New England, and applied them to his career. "Do Your Job" and "No Days Off" are tattooed on his forehead, impressing potential employers.

Now the Digital Marketing Strategist at an Ad Agency, Matt makes a living behind a computer. (Thankfully... Clients don't take kindly to permanent facial ink on someone who's taking their money) He currently develops and manages annoying online advertisements, and couldn't be happier.
